A LETTER TO THE DEVELOPERS

Hi, We Are RTL Play.

We're glad to see you here.

We are a young and ambitious startup working on frameworks for accelerating the design of next-generation of efficient computing systems. At RTL Play, we understand the need for devices and systems that not only perform optimally but also adapt to changing needs and trends. That's where RTL Play comes in - we are pioneering an integrated approach to hardware and software systems design, where the two evolve together, ensuring efficiency, versatility, and technological innovation.

While we are still adding the final touches, we want to share our journey with you. By hitting that Notify Me button, you'll get a front-row seat to our journey and will be among our first crew members to know when we power up and officially launch. We don't do newsletters but we'll be happy to share some sneak peeks into our technology, and even provide you with early bird perks to our services. Meanwhile if you have an exciting project in mind that you want to prototype, let's get in touch! We're eager to hear from you.

Our team of trailblazers are working tirelessly to empower you to design and innovate in ways never before possible. We are set to transform the tech landscape, and we want you to be a part of this revolution!

Stay tuned, because at RTL Play, we are just getting started, and we promise it's going to be an exhilarating ride. The future is co-designed. The future is with RTL Play.

Until then, may the tech force be with you!
